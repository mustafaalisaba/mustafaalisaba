<h2 style="text-align: center;">Death Machines</h2>

<p>
  <span class="initial">W</span>hen he looked into the rearview mirror, he saw madness staring back at him. The thousand-yard stare, lost in the abyss of the pupil. The man began to laugh, but that laughter soon turned into something insane: an agonizing guffaw, laden with fear and despair, as if something had died inside him. He felt an urgent need to escape, to flee from himself. He had loved her for such a short time, and he had lost her forever.
